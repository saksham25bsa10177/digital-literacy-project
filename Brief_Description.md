The presentation explains the concept of digital literacy, which is the ability to confidently and critically find, evaluate, create, and share information using digital technologies such as computers, smartphones, and the internet. It also highlights useful digital tools for students, including GitHub, Google Drive, Google Docs, LinkedIn, Canva, and HackerRank, which help in learning, collaboration, and skill development. Additionally, the presentation emphasizes safe internet practices such as using strong passwords, not sharing OTPs, avoiding unknown links, enabling two-factor authentication, checking website URLs carefully, and avoiding public Wi-Fi for banking to ensure online security.
